How It Works

Up and running in 3 steps

No cloud. No new browser. Just your Chrome, your AI, and your rules.

🔧
Step 01

Install the Chrome Extension + MCP Server

Clone the repo, run the local MCP server, and load the unpacked extension. No accounts, no cloud — everything runs locally on your machine.

🤖
Step 02

Claude / AI takes actions in your real browser

Connect Claude Desktop (or any MCP-compatible AI) to CoDriver's MCP server. The AI sees your real Chrome tabs and can navigate, click, type, and interact — all in your existing session with your cookies and logins.

✅
Step 03

You approve, block, or let it run

Every AI action pauses for your approval (or auto-runs if you trust it). Use the side panel, keyboard shortcuts (Alt+A / Alt+X), or Telegram on your phone. You're always in the driver's seat.

Features

Everything you need to co-pilot

Built for humans who want AI power without losing control.

🔐

Real Chrome Profile

No new browser. No fresh session. AI uses YOUR logged-in Chrome — with all your cookies, saved passwords, and extensions intact.

✅

Approve / Block UI

Every AI action goes through you first. See exactly what the AI wants to do — click, type, navigate — and approve or block with one key.

🛑

CAPTCHA Handoff

AI hits a CAPTCHA? It pauses automatically, you solve it, and the AI resumes right where it left off. Zero friction.

📱

Telegram Alerts

Away from your desk? Approve AI actions from your phone via Telegram. Never miss a step or block a mistake from anywhere.

🗂️

Multi-Tab Control

AI works seamlessly across all your open tabs. Switch context, jump between pages, and manage complex multi-step tasks.

⌨️

Keyboard Shortcuts

Alt+A approve · Alt+X block · Alt+C panel. Lightning-fast control without leaving the keyboard.
